Frequently asked questions

The essentials about Invisyne, integration, deployment, and pilots.

What is Invisyne?

Invisyne creates Industrial Intelligence — it captures machine and asset data continuously and turns it into a reliable understanding of how your machines behave, across the full lifecycle.

Do we have to replace our SCADA, historian, or IIoT system?

No. Invisyne builds on top of your existing connectivity and systems — it adds continuous operational memory and behaviour understanding, it does not replace what you already run.

Which data sources are supported?

Signals are captured directly at the source — Siemens S7, Modbus, OPC UA, and MQTT — up to thousands of variables per second.

On-premise or cloud?

A hybrid model. The Edge devices that capture data always run on-premise at the machine; Hub device management is cloud-based. On request, we can provide an on-premise data infrastructure on a project basis.

How fast can we see value?

Start with one machine and one use case. A focused pilot typically delivers a first reliable analysis within weeks, with no integration cost during the pilot.

Is our data secure and kept separate?

Yes. Hub runs as a single-tenant cloud service and you control access. Edge data is captured on-premise at the machine; for stricter data-residency requirements, an on-premise data infrastructure is available on request.

What does a pilot involve?

One plant, one machine, a defined goal. We connect to your data, build a baseline, and deliver a first analysis with measurable success criteria.

How does Invisyne help with regulatory requirements?

Continuous, timestamped, contextual records make machine behaviour traceable on demand — a solid foundation for audits and evidence.
