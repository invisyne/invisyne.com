Data capture

Edge · Crawler

The digital memory of your machine or asset.

Crawler captures signals directly at the source — Siemens S7, Modbus, OPC UA, MQTT — up to 5,000 variables per second at 50 ms. What used to be lost after a failure remains available as continuous history.

Overview & operations

Hub

The command center for operational knowledge.

Hub manages devices, firmware, users, roles, dashboards, and alerts. Operators and OEMs gain one central view across machines, sites, fields, and asset groups.

Analysis

Deepview

From signals to root causes.

Deepview analyzes high-resolution time series and compares runs, batches, shifts, time windows, or assets. Teams see what changed — and why it matters.

Field

Companion

Make Industrial Intelligence usable in the field.

Companion is the Windows app for commissioning, service, and field work: discover devices, configure, update, and export data — locally on the network, even without cloud access.

The shift

Data shows what happened. Industrial Intelligence explains what it means.

Many companies already have SCADA, historians, IIoT, dashboards, or remote access. But the most important question often remains unanswered: Why does this machine, system, or asset behave differently today than it did yesterday? Invisyne adds continuous operational memory to existing systems and makes behavior, deviations, and root causes understandable in real operations.

Start with a use case that creates measurable value today.

For example: accelerate commissioning, find root causes faster, prevent downtime, preserve knowledge, compare assets, digitize service, or simplify technical evidence. Together, we define the use case, the data foundation, and measurable success criteria.
